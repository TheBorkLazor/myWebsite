About me

Hello, I'm Andrew, but you can call me Andy. I'm currently on the path to becoming a computer programmer, driven by my passion for technology and problem-solving. Beyond coding, I'm a sports enthusiast with a deep love for football and the NFL. Golf, pool, and snooker are my go-to hobbies for unwinding, and I'm also an avid gamer, exploring virtual worlds whenever I can. My current goal is to realize my dream of becoming a software developer. Recently, I embarked on a new adventure, temporarily relocating to broaden my skill set and explore diverse career opportunities. This experience has made me a more flexible, self-motivated, and hard-working individual. My diverse work background includes roles in customer service, communication, amusement ride operation, and bicycle maintenance, where I honed a range of specific and transferable skills. One of the highlights of my journey has been the joy of collaborating with colleagues and building positive working relationships. Now, equipped with these valuable skills, I'm back in the Fylde area, eager to embark on the next phase of my career. Let's connect and explore how we can work together to make the most of the exciting road ahead.
